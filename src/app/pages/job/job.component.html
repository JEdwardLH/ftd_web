{{LanguageText.txt_job_aboutus}}

Food To Dine is a fast and reliable food delivery service in Calamba. We connect people with the best restaurants in the city. We do this by empowering local businesses and in turn, generate new ways for people to earn, work and live. We started by facilitating door-to-door delivery, but we see this as just the beginning of connecting people with possibility — easier evenings, happier days, bigger savings accounts, wider nets and stronger communities.

{{LanguageText.txt_career_keycompetencies}}

We are looking for a high-performing Sales Manager to help us meet our customer acquisition and revenue growth targets by keeping our company competitive and innovative. You will be responsible for maximizing our sales team potential, crafting sales plans and justifying those to plans to the upper management.

  • Achieve growth and hit sales targets by successfully managing the sales team
  • Design and implement a strategic business plan that expands company’s merchant base and ensure it’s strong presence
  • Objectives setting, coaching and performance monitoring of sales representatives
  • Build and promote strong, long-lasting merchant relationships by partnering with them and understanding their needs
  • Present sales, revenue and expenses reports and realistic forecasts to the management team
  • Identify emerging markets and market shifts while being fully aware of new products and competition status
  • Create daily/ weekly in-depth sales report
{{LanguageText.txt_job_respo}}
  • Schedule sales call and sales visit
  • Pitch & win clients for KalanBanga
  • Build & maintain high-level relationships with the merchant
  • Pipeline management, sales forecasting and key sales reports with business KPIs
  • Go through the company's CRM tool to check everything planned for that day is perfectly running.
  • Coordinate with the Marketing team about ongoing promotion
  • Coordinate with the Customer Support team about any new leads on merchants from social media or email.
  • Make a report about achievements & improvements
